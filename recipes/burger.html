Breaded Burger

Rochester Favorite ⭐️

Description:

A breaded burger, also known as a breaded pork tenderloin sandwich, is a regional specialty from the Midwest, particularly popular in states like Iowa and Indiana. It consists of a tenderized and flattened pork tenderloin that is breaded and fried until crispy and golden brown. The breaded pork is typically served on a bun with traditional toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.

The breaded burger is known for its large size, often extending well beyond the edges of the bun. It's a hearty and satisfying sandwich that has become a beloved comfort food in its region of origin. Variations may include different breading styles or toppings, but the essence of a breaded burger lies in its crispy exterior and tender, juicy pork inside. .

    Ingredients

  1. Ground Beef
  2. Oil for frying
  3. Salt, pepper, Adobo, Ancestors blessings, BBQ, Burger Seasoning
  4. Sweet and tangy Rochester style sauce(Optional)
  5. Veggies(Onions,Lettuce,Tomatoes)
  6. Side of Fries

    Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat Over: Set stove to medium-high temp
  2. Prepare Ground Beef: Bread and season Beef with your favorite seasonings
  3. Fry Wings: Deep fry fries until golden brown
  4. Deep dry/AirFry the Spud: Cook Fries to your liking
  5. Toss in Sauce: Mix with Rochester Style sauce
  6. Serve: Enjoy hot!

Time to cook: 45 Minutes

Prepares for: 6 People
