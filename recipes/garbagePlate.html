The Garbage Plate

Rochester Staple ⭐️

A Garbage Plate is a famous dish from Rochester, New York, known for its hearty and indulgent nature. It typically consists of a base of home fries, macaroni salad, or baked beans, topped with various meats such as cheeseburgers, hot dogs, or sausages. The dish is then smothered in a combination of condiments, including mustard, onions, and a meat-based hot sauce.

The Garbage Plate was originally created by Nick Tahou Hots and has become a local favorite, often enjoyed late at night or after a long night out. The dish can be customized with a variety of ingredients, making it a versatile and satisfying meal. Despite its seemingly chaotic appearance, the flavors blend together harmoniously, offering a unique culinary experience.

    Ingredients

  1. Burger Patties
  2. Baked Beans
  3. Fries
  4. Meat Sauce
  5. Ketchup(optional)

    Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare Home Fries: Slice and fry potatoes until golden.
  2. Cook Macaroni Salad: Prepare macaroni salad with mayo and veggies.
  3. Grill Meat: Cook cheeseburgers or hot dogs until done.
  4. Assemble Plate: Layer home fries, macaroni salad, and grilled meat.
  5. Add Toppings: Top with mustard, onions, and hot sauce.
Time to cook: 45 Minutes
Prepares for: 6 People
