Rochester Style Wings

Rochester Style wings are a popular variation of chicken wings from Rochester, New York. Known for their unique preparation and flavor, these wings are typically marinated in a mixture of spices and then grilled or baked, rather than deep-fried. This method gives them a distinct smoky flavor and a slightly crisp texture.

After cooking, the wings are often tossed in a tangy, savory sauce made from a blend of hot sauce, butter, and other seasonings. This gives Rochester Style wings a perfect balance of heat and flavor. They are commonly served with a side of celery and blue cheese dressing, making them a favorite for gatherings and game days.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt, pepper, Adobo, Ancestors blessings, Sazon,
  • Sweet and tangy Rochester style sauce
  • Blue cheese(optional)

    Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat Over:Set stove to medium-high team
  2. Prepare Wings:Bread and season wings with your favorite seasonings
  3. Fry Wings::Deep fry wings until golden brown
  4. Toss in Sauce:Mix wings with Rochester Style sauce
  5. Serve::Enjoy hot!
Time to cook: 35 minutes
Prepares for: 2 People
